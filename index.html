Who am I, you ask?

I am currently a third-year PhD student in the department of statistics (LPSM) at Sorbonne Université, under the supervision of Anna Bonnet (LPSM) and Maxime Sangnier (LPSM).

My PhD is part of the doctoral program of the Sorbonne Center for Artificial Intelligence (SCAI).
